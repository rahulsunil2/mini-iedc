Mad Over Marketing
Effective Marketing Strategy

Case Study: We will give participants case studies and ask them to analyse the case studies and briefly present them as a team of 3 or 2.

Different Marketing Strategies: The participants should then point out the different marketing strategies used and identify the drawbacks and give a solution.

Market an event: Final task is to make use of the different marketing strategies learned and to market an event at college using the same.

Nuclear Hackathon
UI/UX. Front End. Back End.

3 Phases

UI UX: Designers will be developing UI Mockups based on the requirement sheet we provide them.

Front End: Developers can select a UI Mockup which he has to develop. He should develop the Front End in such a way that there is a provision to integrate the APIs.

Back End: Developers should develop the Server as well as the APIs that are needed for the Front End Integration.
